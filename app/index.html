Before you start

TenderProof records your diligence — it never approves carriers. Please confirm:

I am the decision-maker. TenderProof helps me record my carrier-selection diligence. I decide which carriers to use.
It's a recordkeeping tool, not legal advice and not a safety rating. It doesn't tell me a carrier is safe or suitable.
Data is third-party, "as is." Carrier info comes from FMCSA and is stamped with the date retrieved; it may be delayed or incomplete.
The policy is mine. The criteria I adopt or tune are my company's own written standard.
